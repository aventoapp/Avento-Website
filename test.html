About Avento

Avento is a purpose-built platform for planning, capturing, and celebrating events. We combine powerful event management tools with AI-driven photo organization so that hosts and attendees can focus on the moment — not the logistics.

Our product focuses on three core pillars:

  • Delightful UX: A clean, lovable interface that makes event creation and photo contribution painless.
  • Smart Automation: AI groups photos by faces, suggests highlights, and reduces manual sorting.
  • Privacy-first: You own your photos; sharing is opt-in with granular controls.

Founded in 2024 by a small team of designers and engineers passionate about memory-keeping, Avento has grown through customer feedback and a relentless focus on thoughtful details. We ship features incrementally, listen to community requests, and prioritize reliability.

Where we’re headed: richer collaborative editing, improved search across people and places, deeper mobile-first experiences, and integrations that make it effortless to share highlights with friends and social networks while keeping privacy intact.
